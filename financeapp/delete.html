Account & Data Deletion  ·  Mumraiz Malik

Important: Deleting your account permanently removes all your cloud backup data from our servers. This action cannot be undone. Your local data stored on your device is not affected.

Option 1 — Delete Account Directly in the App

The fastest way to delete your account and all associated cloud data is from within Finance App itself. This requires you to be signed in with your Google account.

  1. Open Finance App on your device.
  2. Tap the Backup & Sync tab at the bottom of the screen.
  3. If you are not already signed in, tap Sign in with Google and authenticate.
  4. Under the Account section, tap Delete Account (shown in red below the Sign Out button).
  5. A confirmation dialog will appear explaining what data will be deleted. Tap Yes, Delete My Account to confirm.
  6. Your account and all cloud backups are immediately and permanently deleted.

Option 2 — Submit a Deletion Request by Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion manually, email us at the address below. We will process your request within 30 days.

  1. Send an email to reach.mumraiz@gmail.com with the subject line: "Finance App – Delete My Account".
  2. In the body, include the Google email address linked to your Finance App account so we can identify your data.
  3. We will confirm receipt within 3 business days and complete deletion within 30 days.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

What Data Is Deleted vs. Kept

Data Location On Account Deletion Retention Period
All cloud backup snapshots (financial data) Google Firebase Firestore Permanently deleted Immediately upon deletion
Firebase user document Google Firebase Firestore Permanently deleted Immediately upon deletion
Firebase Authentication account (UID, email, display name) Google Firebase Auth Permanently deleted Immediately upon deletion
Local financial data (transactions, history, recurring items) On-device only (MMKV storage) Kept on device Until app is uninstalled

Local data on your device is never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly use the cloud backup feature. Deleting your account does not erase local device data — to remove that, simply uninstall the app.

Request Data Deletion Without Deleting Your Account

You can delete your cloud backup data without deleting your account. This lets you keep using Finance App with cloud backup while removing all previously stored snapshots.

Option A — Delete individual snapshots in the app

  1. Open Finance App and go to Backup & Sync.
  2. Sign in with Google if prompted.
  3. Under Restore History, tap any snapshot you want to remove. (Currently individual snapshot deletion is managed automatically — the app keeps only the last 10 snapshots and removes older ones automatically.)

Option B — Request cloud data wipe by email

Email us at reach.mumraiz@gmail.com with the subject "Finance App – Delete My Data Only". Include your linked Google email address. We will delete all Firestore snapshots associated with your account while keeping your Firebase Auth account active so you can continue using the app.
