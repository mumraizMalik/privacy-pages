Finance App

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: April 3, 2026

Short version: Your financial data stays on your device by default. Cloud backup is optional and requires your explicit action. We do not sell or share your data with any third parties.

This Privacy Policy describes how Finance App ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about what data we collect and why.

1. Information We Collect

a) Data stored locally (on-device only)

By default, all financial data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Monthly income and expense transactions
  • Recurring budget items and amounts
  • Transaction history and payment statuses

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to enable cloud backup.

b) Data sent to the cloud (optional backup feature)

If you use the Cloud Backup feature, the following is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore:

  • Your complete financial data snapshot (transactions, recurring items, history)
  • Backup timestamp and label
  • A unique anonymous user ID (Firebase UID) — auto-assigned, contains no personal information

A maximum of 10 snapshots are kept; older ones are automatically deleted.

c) Google Account (optional)

You may optionally link your Google account to your cloud backup. If you do, we also store:

  • Your Google email address
  • Your display name
  • Your Google profile photo URL

This is entirely optional and only used to associate backups with your Google identity for cross-device restore.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To store and restore your financial data via cloud backup
  • To identify your backup snapshots in Firebase
  • To display your name and profile photo in the app (if Google account is linked)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Cloud data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, secured by Firebase Security Rules
  • Only you (authenticated user) can read or write your own data
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Local data is stored using MMKV secure local storage on your device

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (Firestore & Authentication) — for cloud backup and optional Google Sign-In.
    firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Sign-In — used optionally to link your Google identity to your backup.
    policies.google.com/privacy

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services.

5. Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained until you uninstall the app
  • Cloud backups: Retained until you delete them or unlink your account (max 10 snapshots)
  • Anonymous Firebase account: Retained unless you request deletion

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Disable cloud backup anytime — data stays only on your device
  • Delete individual backup snapshots from within the app
  • Unlink your Google account from within the app
  • Request full deletion of your cloud data by contacting us
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
